What I Bring to the Table

I Build Digital Products That Are Fast, Secure & Built to Scale.

From consumer-facing interfaces to strengthened back-end systems, I develop full-stack web applications with a strong emphasis on hardened security — think rate-limiting, input sanitisation, role-based access control, and end-to-end encrypted data pipelines. Your users' data is never an afterthought.

Database wise, I design schemas that are both quick and resilient against breakdowns — parameterised queries to minimize injection attacks and automated backups.

I pair that technical rigour with a sharp eye for design: clean, responsive UIs that load fast, feel effortless, and leave a lasting impression — because a secure product should also be a beautiful one.
